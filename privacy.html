What's Normal

Privacy Policy - Last updated March 2026

Overview

Privacy first

What's Normal is designed with your privacy as a priority. We collect as little data as possible and process everything on your device whenever we can.

What We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers. The following data may be handled by Apple on our behalf:

  • Purchase information — Handled entirely by Apple's App Store and StoreKit. We never see your payment details.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics — Only if you have opted in to share diagnostics with Apple in your device settings.

On-Device AI

Fully offline

The Flight Support chat feature uses Apple's Foundation Models framework. All AI processing happens entirely on your device. Your conversations are never sent to any server — not ours, not Apple's AI servers. Chat history is saved locally in your device's storage and can be cleared at any time from within the app.

App Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using UserDefaults:

  • Your completed fear topics and badge progress
  • Accessibility preferences (larger text, reduce motion, etc.)
  • Chat message history
  • Free message usage count

This data never leaves your device unless you use iCloud backup, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third Parties

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only third-party service involved is Apple's App Store for processing subscription purchases, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal data on our servers, there is no data to delete or export from our side. To delete all local app data, simply delete the app from your device. Your purchase history is managed by Apple and can be reviewed in your Apple ID settings.

Children's Privacy

What's Normal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
